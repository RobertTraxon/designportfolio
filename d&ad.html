This project was a submission for the 2016 D&AD New Blood Awards. Often seen as the holy grail of design and advertising, D&AD put out a selection of intricate briefs each year for young designers around the world to submit work for. I managed to pick up the top prize of a yellow pencil, proving myself capable of being able to respond to briefs creatively and effectively.
I chose the Shutterstock brief which asked to represent 3 of the archetypes of storytelling through either an image or 20 second animation. Unfamiliar with animation, I chose to challenge myself and create 3 short a videos that would provide a strong moral message within them.
Each animation used a 8-bit game style to reflect issues of the past through a different archetype of storytelling. This was to highlight how the stories we hear today of our history, still have relevance to the issues we find in todayâ€™s society.
