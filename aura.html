Calming & Balanced

The goal of the Aura landing page was to reflect the calm and relaxed nature of the business through the layout and colours selected. I focused on a minimalistic feel with plenty of white space and a natural flow between elements to slowly introduce the customer to the brand. This was all to try and emulate the purpose of their products within the site itself.

Mobile First

With over 60% of traffic to e-commerce sites coming from mobile, it was important to take a mobile-first approach to the design here. This allowed me to work within the guidelines of the bootstrap CSS system, and then sizing up for desktop and tablet meant I could utilise a more spacious feel.
